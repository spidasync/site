Crosshair Games is run by a 16-year-old from Australia, focusing on game development for fun.

Crosshair Games specializes in VHS and Body-Cam styled horror games, so future projects will likely continue this theme.

Since game development is mostly a hobby, Crosshair Games will most likely keep future games free of charge.
